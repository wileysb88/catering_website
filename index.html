ABOUT US

That's the question that sparked Viet Nom Nom, a Vietnamese food startup just launched by Northwestern Kellogg alum Noah Bleicher. His aim is to combine the fast-casual food style of Chipotle with fresh, healthy dishes from another side of the world, targeting the new generation of health-conscious foodies on the go. While in an entrepreneurship workshop, Bleicher and his fellow business school students bemoaned the fact that the only options for quick, cheap food are usually laden with fat, sugar, and salt. "Why, if you're driving home late at night, can you only get McDonalds? Why isn't there something you can [eat] and actually feel good about yourself after you eat it?" he said.

ABOUT OUR FOOD

Viet Nom Nom was built on the philosophy that fast food can also be good food. We believe Vietnamese cuisine provides the best example of combining fresh, healthy ingredients with complex and delicious flavors. Vietnamese food uses many raw, unprocessed vegetables and herbs, which maintains both natural flavors and the nutrition content that you’d otherwise lose through cooking. The proteins we use are never fried but instead always grilled or baked, which allows the true flavors to come out and not be hidden by a heavy, unhealthy batter. Fresh, flavorful, filling. These are the words that encapsulate Viet Nom Nom’s menu and mission with our food. We want you to be able to taste the freshness, feel healthier for eating it, and be satisfied throughout your day. At Viet Nom Nom, we believe in “feel-good-food” and want to share the deliciousness with you!
